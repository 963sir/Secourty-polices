SIR | Security is committed to protecting user privacy while providing moderation, server protection, and safety features on Discord.

Data Collected:
The bot only processes the minimum data necessary to operate its moderation and protection features. This may include Discord user IDs, server IDs, role data, channel IDs, message content required for moderation checks, and audit log data when needed for moderation or security actions.

Data Usage:
This data is used solely to enforce server rules, detect spam and repeated messages, identify malicious or prohibited links, support moderation workflows, and provide moderation logs for authorized server staff.

Data Storage & Retention:
Most message content is processed in real time for moderation purposes and is not permanently stored off-platform by the bot. Limited server configuration data, such as moderation settings, log channel settings, and protection preferences, may be stored locally to maintain bot functionality. Temporary moderation-related data may also be processed in memory or short-term runtime storage and cleared automatically when no longer needed.

Moderation Logs:
When moderation logging is enabled by a server, relevant moderation information may be sent to designated Discord log channels that are accessible only to authorized server staff, based on that server’s permissions and configuration.

No Third-Party Sharing:
We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data is only disclosed if required for legal compliance or to respond to valid legal requests.

User Rights:
Server owners or affected users may contact us to ask questions about how data is processed or to request review or deletion where applicable.

Contact:
For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us on Discord at @963Sir.
